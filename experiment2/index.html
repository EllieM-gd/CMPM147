Experiment X - Subject/Topic

Description

In this experiment I attempted to recreate a picture of palm trees on a beach during a sunset. I use a lot of randomness to generate the picture so that the tree position, sand, ocean, and sunset look different every time.

Technical

The code first determines the size of the beach and places pixels with 1 of 4 colors in the designated area to create the beach. I do something similar, except its all one random blue color, for the ocean above it. I also use this time to generate a random amount of waves inside the ocean. I then lastly generate a random gradient background and use that for the sky color. After this I update the pixels on the canvas and move on to the sun which is a slightly random eclipse that appears on the left or right of the screen. Then clouds are created in front of it. The clouds are a bunch of eclipses that are supposed to be grey on the bottom and be a similar color to the sky behind them on top. Lastly I create palm trees on the beach which is just a thick line, with a trapezoid on the bottom, some coconuts and leaves that are rotated around the tip and the bottom ones are more brown like my reference photo.

Reflection

I'm pretty proud of this project, even though it looks very simple I had a lot of fun making it. I think I was able to capture a small essence of the original photo I was basing this off of.
